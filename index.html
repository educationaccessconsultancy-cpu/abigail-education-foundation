Meet Our Founder

Dr. James K. Mushi, PhD

Founder / Director

Dr. James K. Mushi founded the Abigail Eduaccess Shule Foundation with a vision to ensure that no child is left behind due to lack of basic school necessities. With a deep commitment to education and community development, he leads the foundation in its mission to provide shoes, socks, uniforms, and stationery to children in need.

Under his leadership, the foundation has grown to support hundreds of children across multiple schools in Tanzania, creating lasting change one child at a time through the Shoes for School Initiative and the Dignity Initiative.

The foundation also runs an Education Access Advisory Program that engages communities on the streets or walking to school who lack basic school supplies, providing immediate support where resources permit.
