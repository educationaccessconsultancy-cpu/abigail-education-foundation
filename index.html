🤝 Street Outreach

Street Outreach Educational Support

Direct engagement model for immediate impact.

Reaching Children Where They Are

The foundation adopts a direct engagement model, identifying children encountered on the streets or walking to school who lack basic school supplies.

Immediate support is provided where resources permit, ensuring rapid response to urgent need.

"Every child equipped. Every child included. Every child learning."
